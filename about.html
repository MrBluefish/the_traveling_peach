About The Traveling Peach

The Traveling Peach is a woman-owned mobile beauty boutique. I handpick quality cosmetics and perfumes for busy women and moms, then bring the shopping experience right to their homes through fun ladies’ nights and parties. It’s a modern twist on the old-fashioned traveling salesman—convenient, personal, and social.
